Pay attention to the colored circles. This is the most important part.

At this point you have to pay attention to the color and location of a set of colored circles. These circles will appear on the screen for a very brief period before turning gray, flicker or disappear completely. Try to not look directly at one of them to avoid missing features of some of the other circles. The best strategy is to focus on the center of the screen, even if you think this is counter intuitive.
